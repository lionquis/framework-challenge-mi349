Cafe Laneau

Artisanal coffee roastery & cafe in Laeken, Brussels

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Cafe Laneau is a small-batch coffee roastery and cafe located in the Laeken district of Brussels. We are passionate about sourcing high-quality beans from sustainable farms around the world and roasting them on-site in our cafe to bring out their unique flavors. Our cozy cafe offers a welcoming atmosphere where coffee lovers can enjoy expertly crafted beverages and delicious pastries.

What we offer

  • Specialty coffee drinks made with our freshly roasted beans
  • A selection of pastries and light snacks
  • Whole bean coffee for home brewing
  • Drinks made with care and passion
  • Workshops and tastings to deepen your coffee knowledge
